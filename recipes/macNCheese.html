Mac n Cheese

Description

Homeade Mac n Cheese with Velveeta cheese and milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni, uncooked
  • 12 ounces Velveeta cheese (cut into 1/2 inch cubes)
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to directions on box then drain well.
  2. Return pasta to pot and add cheese, milk, and pepper. Stir constantly over low heat until cheese is melted.
  3. Serve and enjoy.
