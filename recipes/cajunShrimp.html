Cajun Shrimp

Description

Sheet pan dinner that includes potatoes, roasted garlic, corn on the cob and Shrimp with a butter and old bay seasoning sprinkled on top.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds baby red or multicolored potatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 ears corn, husked and cut crosswise into 1-inch rounds
